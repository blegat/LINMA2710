\question{}

Consider the FTCS (forward time, centered space) scheme for the following diffusion problem:
\begin{align*}
& u_t(t,x) = \kappa \, u_{xx}(t,x), \quad x\in[0,1], t\geq0, 
\\ & u(t,0) = 0 = u(t,1) \quad \text{for all $t\geq0$},
\\ & u(0,x) = u^0(x) \quad \text{for all $x\in[0,1]$}.
\end{align*}

\begin{parts}

\part Write the FTCS scheme, including the boundary and initial conditions. 

\part Briefly give the meaning of each symbol (excluding the basic arithmetic, of course) that you have introduced. You can use a mix of words and equations.

\part Explain the notion of \emph{refinement path}.

\part Give (without proof) a refinement path along which the FTCS scheme is stable. Explain what this means.

\part Give (without proof) a refinement path along which the FTCS scheme is \emph{not} stable. Explain what this means.

\end{parts}
